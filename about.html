About Us

Welcome to the Department of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) at Veer Narmad South Gujarat University. Our department is a hub of innovation, research, and quality education, shaping the future of ICT professionals.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in Information and Communication Technology, fostering innovation, research, and societal transformation.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education, encourage research, and develop competent professionals with ethical values and global perspectives.

Our History

Established in 2004, the ICT department has grown to become a leader in technology education in South Gujarat, producing skilled graduates who excel worldwide.
