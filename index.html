Welcome to the Department of ICT

The Department of Information and Communication Technology at Veer Narmad South Gujarat University is dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation in the field of Information Technology. Our programs are designed to prepare students for careers in software development, networking, data analytics, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies.

We offer state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and a student-friendly environment that encourages creativity and practical learning. Join us to explore your potential and shape the future of ICT.
